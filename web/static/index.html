1. Fully-loaded cost per GPU-hour

Depreciation + power (scaled by PUE) + facility opex. Provisioned cost = cost of owning the GPU for an hour regardless of load; billable cost = provisioned cost / utilization.

2. Cost per 1M tokens (inference)

Cheapest generation first. Effective tokens/hr = tokens/sec × 3600 × utilization.

3. Gross margin at on-demand price

Revenue earned on billable hours vs cost incurred on provisioned hours.

4. Depreciation useful-life sensitivity

5. Reserved vs on-demand break-even

Above what utilization does committing to a reservation beat paying on-demand?

6. Rent vs buy at live market prices

Owning costs the provisioned rate every hour; renting costs the market rate only on utilized hours. Rental $/hr uses the cheapest live quote per GPU (falls back to the on-demand assumption). Solid = own, dashed = rent.
